About Me

I am a power system engineer with over 8 years of experience, passionate about creating a stable and efficient energy future.
My expertise lies in conducting comprehensive power system studies, integrating renewable energy systems, and spearheading research and development initiatives.

I thrive on guiding and mentoring study teams to address critical operational and planning challenges faced by transmission utilities, generating stations, and renewable energy projects. Through my leadership, I guide and mentor a team of engineers in conducting power system studies, planning activities, and grid interconnection studies. My goal is to foster a collaborative environment where innovation and knowledge sharing flourish, ensuring optimal solutions for every project.

I believe in a future powered by clean and sustainable energy sources.
